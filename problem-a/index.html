Haiji Kiyose

Haiji Kiyose is from the Japanese Television Series: Run with the Wind (Kaze ga Tsuyoku Fuiteiru). He is a senior and the leader of Kansei University's track and field club. With a strong spirit, he is well loved by watchers of the show.

I like Haiji Kiyose for 3 reasons:

  • He always thinks about the members on his team
  • He respects everyone around him
  • He is driven to reach his goal
